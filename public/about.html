Our Heritage

OGBO AND SONS NIGERIA LIMITED was officially established on 04 March 1996 in Umuahia, Abia State, Nigeria. What began as a small-scale cocoa trading operation has evolved into one of Nigeria's most respected and trusted cocoa export companies.

For nearly three decades, we have remained deeply rooted in Nigeria's cocoa belt, building strong relationships with farmers and sourcing the finest Grade 1 cocoa beans from the country's richest agricultural regions. Our journey from regional sourcing to structured export programs reflects our commitment to meeting international standards, certifications, and the ever-evolving expectations of global buyers.

From our headquarters in Umuahia, we have developed a reputation for reliability, premium quality, and professional excellence. Today, we proudly serve clients across more than 25 countries, delivering cocoa products that consistently meet and exceed global benchmarks.

At the core of our operations is a steadfast commitment to sustainability and ethical practices. We invest in farmer training, fair financing, and post-harvest best practices to protect both yields and livelihoods. Through strict quality control—covering fermentation, moisture levels, and traceability—we ensure our cocoa meets the demands of discerning chocolate manufacturers and industrial buyers worldwide.
